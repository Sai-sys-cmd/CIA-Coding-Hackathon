What is Ocean Pollution?

Ocean pollution refers to the contamination of the ocean environment by harmful substances such as plastic, chemicals, and waste. It harms marine life and ecosystems.

Fun Fact: Approximately 8 million tons of plastic end up in the ocean each year!

The Impact on Marine Life

Marine animals ingest plastics, leading to injury or death. Coral reefs, one of the most diverse ecosystems, are also being destroyed due to pollution.

Not only does this harm marine life, but it also affects human life by disrupting the food chain and harming the economy.
